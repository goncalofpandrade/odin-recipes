Tofu à Gomes de Sá

Description

Another transformation of a Portuguese codfish dish to a vegan "codfish" dish

Ingredients

  • 500g of potatoes;
  • 1 onion;
  • 1 leek stalk;
  • 4 garlic cloves;
  • 2 bay leaves;
  • 250g of tofu;
  • Enough soy sauce;
  • Enough garlic powder, paprika, oregano and black pepper;
  • ½ sprig of parsley;
  • Enough olives;
  • Enough salt;
  • Enough olive oil.

Steps

  1. Wash the potatoes well and cook them (with the skin) in salted water, drain, remove the skin and cut them into quarters;
  2. Thinly slice the onion and leek and mince the garlic. In a frying pan with a drizzle of olive oil, sauté the onion, leek, garlic and bay leaf for a few minutes. Remove and reserve;
  3. Cut the tofu into cubes. Place in a frying pan and season with a drizzle of olive oil, soy sauce, garlic powder, paprika, oregano and black pepper, stirring well. Bring to a boil and sauté for 5 minutes taste and rectify the seasoning;
  4. Transfer the potato, tofu and braised onion to an oven dish and drizzle with olive oil;
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 200ºC for about 20 minutes or until golden brown. Remove and stir in the chopped parsley and olives. Serve hot.
