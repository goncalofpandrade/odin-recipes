Tofu à Gomes de Sá

Description

Another transformation of a Portuguese codfish dish to a vegan "codfish" dish

Ingredients

  • 500g of potatoes;
  • 1 onion;
  • 1 leek stalk;
  • 4 garlic cloves;
  • 2 bay leaves;
  • 250g of tofu;
  • Enough soy sauce;
  • Enough garlic powder, paprika, oregano and black pepper;
  • ½ sprig of parsley;
  • Enough olives;
  • Enough salt;
  • Enough olive oil.
