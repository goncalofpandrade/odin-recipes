Tofu à Brás

Description

A transformation of a Portuguese codfish dish to a vegan "codfish" dish

Ingredients

  • 200g baking potatoes or sweet potatoes;
  • 250g firm tofu;
  • 120g of mushrooms;
  • 1 leek;
  • 1 small onion;
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil;
  • Chopped coriander to taste;
  • Black olives to taste;
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce;
  • Turmeric powder to taste;
  • Garlic powder to taste;
  • Black pepper to taste;
  • Nutmeg to taste.

Steps

  1. Wash the potatoes very well and dry with a cloth (use with the skin);
  2. With a mandoline, cut the potato into thin sticks;
  3. Season the potato sticks with garlic powder, black pepper, nutmeg, turmeric powder and a drizzle of olive oil;
  4. Arrange the sticks on a tray lined with parchment paper and bake at 180 degrees for 30 minutes (stir every 10 minutes to avoid burning);
  5. In a saucepan, sauté the sliced onion with a drizzle of olive oil until golden;
  6. Add the leek cut into thin slices and let it cook for 1 minute;
  7. Chop the mushrooms and add to the stew. Let it cook for 3 to 4 minutes (until reduced in volume);
  8. With a fork, crush the tofu and add to the mushrooms;
  9. Season with soy sauce, black pepper, nutmeg and turmeric powder. Engage everything very well and turn off the fire;
  10. Add the potatoes and add the chopped coriander and mix;
  11. Finally, decorate with black olives.
